January: Who and What Are We?

Who we are as the body of Christ in 2019 has everything to do with the wisdom and sacrifices of those who came before us. Unfortunately, we often live as if the contemporary church were an island in time, cut of from the influence of the past and without regard for its impact on the future. So, join us this month as we learn the value of church history and explore our personal spiritual roots.

My Spiritual Family Tree

Think about the people that shaped your walk with God. In what ways does your faith look like theirs? What beliefs and behaviors did you inherit from them? Download this Spiritual Family Tree to record the names of those who have influenced your walk of faith. When finished, you can save and frame this document to create a special family heirloom.

Let’s Pray…

Help us, Lord, to be humble and curious as we look beyond our personal experience of faith in the here and now. Show us how You’ve been working in ways, places, and people that are foreign to us. Guide us, Holy Spirit, as You open our eyes to the history of the faithful.
